Organic Farming Course

Learn sustainable farming practices that promote soil health, biodiversity, and chemical-free food production.

Course Overview

This free course will teach you the principles and practices of organic farming. You'll learn how to grow food without synthetic chemicals, improve soil fertility naturally, and manage pests organically.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Organic Farming
    Understand the fundamentals of organic agriculture and its benefits for health and environment.
  • Soil Health and Management
    Learn about composting, green manures, and other techniques to build healthy soil.
  • Organic Pest and Disease Control
    Discover natural methods to manage pests and diseases without chemicals.
  • Crop Rotation and Diversity
    Learn how crop rotation and polycultures improve yields and reduce pests.
  • Organic Fertilizers and Amendments
    Explore various organic fertilizers like compost tea, bone meal, and seaweed extracts.
  • Weed Management
    Learn organic weed control techniques including mulching and manual removal.
  • Organic Certification
    Understand the process and requirements for organic certification.
  • Marketing Organic Products
    Learn how to market and sell organic produce at premium prices.

Who Should Enroll?

This course is ideal for farmers transitioning to organic methods, home gardeners, agricultural students, and anyone interested in sustainable food production.
