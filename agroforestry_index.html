Agroforestry and Tree-Based Farming Course

Learn how to integrate trees and shrubs into farming systems for environmental and economic benefits.

Course Overview

This free course is designed to provide you with the knowledge and skills needed to implement agroforestry practices. You will learn about the benefits of agroforestry, tree-crop interactions, and sustainable land management.

Course Content

  • Introduction to Agroforestry
    Learn the basics of agroforestry, its history, and its role in sustainable agriculture.
  • Benefits of Agroforestry
    Understand how agroforestry improves soil health, biodiversity, and farm productivity.
  • Tree-Crop Interactions
    Explore the relationships between trees and crops, including competition and mutual benefits.
  • Silvopasture Systems
    Learn how to integrate trees, livestock, and forage for sustainable grazing systems.
  • Alley Cropping
    Discover how to grow crops between rows of trees for improved soil and crop yields.
  • Windbreaks and Shelterbelts
    Understand how to use trees to protect crops and soil from wind erosion.
  • Carbon Sequestration and Climate Mitigation
    Learn how agroforestry contributes to carbon sequestration and climate change mitigation.
  • Designing Agroforestry Systems
    Explore practical steps to design and implement agroforestry systems on your farm.

Who Should Enroll?

This course is ideal for farmers, landowners, agricultural students, and anyone interested in sustainable land management and agroforestry practices.
